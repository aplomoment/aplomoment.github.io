What is the Alberta Child Care Subsidy?

The Alberta Child Care Subsidy is a government program designed to provide financial assistance to families who require child care services. This subsidy aims to make child care more affordable and accessible, ensuring families can secure quality care for their children.

Who is Eligible for the Alberta Child Care Subsidy?

To be eligible for the Alberta Child Care Subsidy, families must meet specific criteria, including:

  • Residing in Alberta.
  • Having a child under 13 years of age.
  • Working, attending school, or participating in an approved training program.

How to Apply for the Alberta Child Care Subsidy

Applying for the Alberta Child Care Subsidy is a straightforward process:

  1. Gather Necessary Documents: Assemble documents such as proof of income, child’s birth certificate, and child care provider information.

  2. Complete the Application: Access the application form online or obtain it from a Family Resource Centre. Fill it out accurately and completely.

  3. Submit the Application: Submit the completed application form, along with the required documents, to the appropriate government office.
