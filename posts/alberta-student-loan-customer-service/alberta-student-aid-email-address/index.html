FAQ#

Q1: What is the Alberta Student Aid email address?#

A1: The Alberta Student Aid email address is asa.clientservices@gov.ab.ca. This email address is dedicated to providing students with information and support regarding their student aid applications, payments, and any other related inquiries.

Q2: When can I contact the Alberta Student Aid email address for assistance?#

A2: The Alberta Student Aid email address is available to answer student inquiries during regular business hours, which are Monday to Friday, 8:15 AM to 4:30 PM (MST). Students are encouraged to contact the email address during these hours to receive prompt assistance.

Q3: What kind of inquiries can I send to the Alberta Student Aid email address?#

A3: Students can send a variety of inquiries related to their student aid to the Alberta Student Aid email address. This includes questions about eligibility, application forms, deadlines, payment schedules, refunds, and any other concerns they may have regarding their student financial assistance.

Q4: How long will it take to receive a response to my email inquiry?#

A4: The Alberta Student Aid team strives to respond to all email inquiries within two business days. However, during peak periods, such as the beginning of the academic year or application deadlines, there may be a slight delay in response times. Students are advised to be patient and allow sufficient time for a response.

Q5: Are there any other ways to contact Alberta Student Aid for assistance?#

A5: In addition to the Alberta Student Aid email address, students can also contact the office through phone or mail. The phone number is 780-422-0306 (Edmonton area) or 1-800-661-2526 (toll-free in Alberta). The mailing address is Alberta Student Aid, 10030-107 Street NW, Edmonton, AB T5J 3E2.
