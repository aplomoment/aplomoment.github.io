Alberta Student Loan Customer Service

Navigating the complexities of student loans can be a daunting task, especially when you’re facing challenges or uncertainties. Alberta Student Loan customer service representatives are dedicated to providing support and guidance to help you understand and manage your student loan journey.

How to Contact Alberta Student Loan Customer Service

  • Phone: Call the Alberta Student Loan customer service line at 1-866-428-9962. The line is open from Monday to Friday between 8:00 am to 5:00 pm.
  • Email: Send an email to [customerexperience@studentaid.alberta.ca] with your inquiry or concern. Emails are typically responded to within 2 business days.
  • Mail: You can also send a letter to:

Alberta Student Loan Service PO Box 4403, Station South Edmonton, AB T6E 6C2

  • In Person: If you prefer to speak to a representative in person, you can visit the Alberta Student Loan office located at 10220 - 103 Avenue NW, 5th Floor, Edmonton, AB T5J 3M3. Walk-in appointments are accepted during office hours, Monday to Friday from 8:30 am to 4:30 pm.

Information to Have on Hand

When contacting Alberta Student Loan customer service, make sure you have the following information readily available:

  • Your Student Loan Number: This is a unique identifier assigned to your student loan account. You can find it on your loan statement or in your online account.
  • Your Social Insurance Number (SIN): This is required for identification purposes.
  • Any Relevant Documents: If you’re inquiring about a specific issue, such as a change in your financial situation or a request for deferment, having the relevant documents on hand will help to expedite the process.

What to Expect from the Interaction

When you contact Alberta Student Loan customer service, you can expect to receive friendly and professional assistance. The representatives are trained to handle a wide range of inquiries and are committed to providing accurate and timely information.

Wait times can vary depending on the time of day and the volume of calls, so it’s a good idea to call during off-peak hours if possible. You can also use the online inquiry form to submit your question or concern, which may result in a faster response time.

Tips for a Productive Interaction

To make the most of your interaction with Alberta Student Loan customer service, here are a few tips:

  • Be Prepared: Have all the necessary information and documents ready before you contact customer service. This will help to ensure that your inquiry is handled efficiently.
  • Be Clear and Concise: When explaining your issue or concern, be clear and concise. Provide all the relevant details, but avoid rambling or going off-topic.
  • Be Patient: Remember that the customer service representatives are handling multiple inquiries simultaneously. Be patient and understanding if you have to wait on hold or if the representative needs time to research your issue.
  • Be Polite and Respectful: Treat the customer service representatives with courtesy and respect. Remember that they are there to help you, and being rude or abusive will not expedite the process.
